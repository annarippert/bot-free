Bot settings

Manual control or Bot Free's Ml automatic decision-making?

In "Manual" mode, you will have the authority to decide how to handle each flagged bot activity. On the other hand, in "Automatic" mode, the bot activity will be promptly addressed based on decisions made by machine learning algorithms.

Manual
Automatic

Bot activity to be deleted or hidden from your account(s)?

If you select "delete," the activity will be permanently removed and will not be accessible from any account; it will completely disappear. On the other hand, if you choose "hide," the activity will not be visible to you or others, except for the account on which the comment was originally created.

Delete
Hide

Automatically report accounts?

Bot activity that engages in harmful or abusive behavior, spread false information, violate community guidelines, or participate in any other form of inappropriate or malicious activity are recommended to be reported. It's essential to report such accounts to maintain a safe and positive online environment for all users.
